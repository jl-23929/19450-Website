Our Team

Finn Silverston

Designer

This is my third season in FTC. After generating a robust insight into coding within FTC, I elected to transfer to CAD design throughout this year. I have been refining my CAD skills, becoming proficient within both Fusion and OnShape. Within the CAD I have mainly tackled tasks such as organisation and efficiency within the robot and mechanisms.

Thomas

Designer

I am one of the main CAD designers and this season have been dedicating the majority of my time to designing and iterating upon the intake, a new endeavour I have not uptaken in the past. This season I have made major changes to my CADing specialties switching to Onshape in order to take a more collaborative approach to design and hopefully improve productivity and quality through peer review and equal distribution of workload.

Shiv

Management

This is my fourth season in FTC robotics and being a part of Team 19450. I have been lucky enough to experience the national stage twice in my last three seasons of FTC and am eager to make a mark on the international stage. I am a proficient engineer currently focusing on extending myself within the outreach and CAD sector. I have been able to solo lead sponsorship agreements this seasons with a variety of companies, initiating contact and collaboration whilst spreading the name of FIRST and FTC.
